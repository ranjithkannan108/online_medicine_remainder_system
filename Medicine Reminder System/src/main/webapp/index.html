About
What is MedReminder?

MedReminder is an online medicine reminder system designed to help people — especially the elderly — manage their medication schedules effectively and never miss a dose.

Many people forget to take medicines on time, leading to health complications. Our system solves this by sending timely alerts, tracking medicine stock, and maintaining a complete history of doses taken.

Built with a clean, accessible interface that works on both desktop and mobile devices.
